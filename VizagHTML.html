Vizag: The City of Destiny!

Rushikonda Beach

Bordered by emerald greenery and sparkling blue waters, the virgin beach of Rushikonda is a soothing sight to your sore eyes and tired souls. The waters of Rushikonda are as blue as it can get and you will be hard pressed to find a beach this soothing to the senses. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Vizag. Set in the midst of lush vegetation adjacent to the shoreline, it is known for its mesmerizing scenery. The beach is a favorite among water sports lovers. Being a beach on the east coast, it is a pleasure to see the sunrise from here and the sunsets are equally dreamy. • Food: Lip-smacking delicacies that are sold in the hill-top restaurants with mesmerizing views of the sea. • Leisure Activities: Jet skiing, windsurfing, speed boating, scuba diving, snorkeling, swimming, and sea kayaking. (Much of these are halted during monsoons.) • Bonus: As the sun bids adieu for the day, the place transforms into a wonderful spot suitable for quiet evening walks.

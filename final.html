Vizag: The City of Destiny!

Rushikonda Beach

Bordered by emerald greenery and sparkling blue waters, the virgin beach of Rushikonda is a soothing sight to your sore eyes and tired souls. The waters of Rushikonda are as blue as it can get and you will be hard pressed to find a beach this soothing to the senses. It is one of the cleanest beaches in Vizag. Set in the midst of lush vegetation adjacent to the shoreline, it is known for its mesmerizing scenery. The beach is a favorite among water sports lovers. Being a beach on the east coast, it is a pleasure to see the sunrise from here and the sunsets are equally dreamy. • Food: Lip-smacking delicacies that are sold in the hill-top restaurants with mesmerizing views of the sea. • Leisure Activities: Jet skiing, windsurfing, speed boating, scuba diving, snorkeling, swimming, and sea kayaking. (Much of these are halted during monsoons.) • Bonus: As the sun bids adieu for the day, the place transforms into a wonderful spot suitable for quiet evening walks.

Ramakrishna Beach

Among the most prominent Vizag beaches names, Rama Krishna Beach aka RK Beach has a charm of its own. It is a spectacular stretch along the Bay of Bengal shoreline, something you shouldn’t miss out on visiting while in Vizag. Thanks to its beauty and popularity, this part of the seafront witnesses constant footfall from tourists and locals alike. While swimming in the waters of this beach is not really safe, you can indulge in various water sports. What more could a beach lover ask for? Additionally, there are a number of boats and ships operating on one side of the shore and one can take a local cruise to enjoy the beautiful view of the sunset. • Prime Attractions: Kursura Submarine Museum, ‘Victory at Sea’ war memorial. • Leisure Activities: Windsurfing, water surfing, boating, beach volleyball, etc. • Other activities: Camel rides, ride in a traditional fishing boat arranges by fishers. • Food: Roadside eateries and parks in the vicinity of the beach make it a great spot for family outings.
